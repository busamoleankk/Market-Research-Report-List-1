<p><p>The Optical Distribution Frame (ODF) market consists of three primary types: Wall Mount ODF, Floor Mount ODF, and Rack Mount ODF. Wall Mount ODFs are compact and suitable for limited space, ideal for small installations. Floor Mount ODFs offer greater capacity and can accommodate higher fiber counts, making them suitable for larger networks. Rack Mount ODFs are designed for integration into standard server racks, facilitating efficient fiber management in data centers. Each type caters to specific deployment needs in telecommunications and data infrastructure.</p></p>
