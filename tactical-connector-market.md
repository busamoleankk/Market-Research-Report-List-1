<p><p>The Tactical Connector Market focuses on two primary types of fiber: Single-Mode (SM) and Multi-Mode (MM). SM fiber connectors are designed for long-distance communication, facilitating higher bandwidth and lower attenuation due to their smaller core diameter. In contrast, MM fiber connectors are ideal for shorter distances, offering higher bandwidth over limited ranges with a larger core diameter. Both types are critical for military and tactical applications, ensuring reliable communication in demanding environments, each serving specific operational needs based on distance and data requirements.</p></p>
