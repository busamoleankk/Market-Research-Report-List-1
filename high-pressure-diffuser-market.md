<p><p>The high-pressure diffuser market is segmented into two main types: with filter and without filter. Diffusers with filters are designed to separate particles and impurities from the air or fluid being diffused, ensuring enhanced quality and efficiency, commonly used in industrial and HVAC applications. On the other hand, diffusers without filters focus on straightforward diffusion without additional filtration mechanisms, often used in applications where pre-filtered air is already available, resulting in lower maintenance costs and simpler designs.</p></p>
