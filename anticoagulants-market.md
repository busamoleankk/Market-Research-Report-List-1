<p><p>The anticoagulants market is segmented into several types: </p><p>1. **Platelet Aggregation Inhibitors (PAI)** prevent platelet clumping, reducing clot formation.</p><p>2. **Low-Molecular-Weight Heparin (LMWH)** offers a predictable anticoagulation effect with fewer side effects.</p><p>3. **Direct Thrombin Inhibitors (DTI)** directly inhibit thrombin, preventing fibrin formation.</p><p>4. **Direct Factor Xa Inhibitors (DFXa)** specifically target Factor Xa, crucial in the coagulation cascade.</p><p>5. **Vitamin K Antagonists (VKA)** inhibit vitamin K, essential for clotting factor production.</p><p>6. **Others** include emerging and niche anticoagulants, expanding treatment options.</p></p>
