<p><p>The articulated welding robot market is primarily segmented into two main types: spot welding robots and arc welding robots. Spot welding robots are designed for high-speed, precise joining of metal parts through localized heat generated by electrical current, making them ideal for automotive and manufacturing sectors. In contrast, arc welding robots employ a continuous arc between an electrode and the workpiece, allowing for versatile applications in various materials and thicker components, suitable for heavy industries and construction.</p></p>
