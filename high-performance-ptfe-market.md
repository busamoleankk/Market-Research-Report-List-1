<p><p>The High Performance PTFE market consists of three main types: Granular PTFE, Aqueous Dispersion PTFE, and Micronized PTFE. Granular PTFE is utilized primarily in high-temperature applications due to its excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance. Aqueous Dispersion PTFE offers advantages in coatings and surface treatments, providing uniform application characteristics. Micronized PTFE is used for enhancing the properties of various formulations, such as adhesives and sealants, by improving lubrication and wear resistance, thus catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
