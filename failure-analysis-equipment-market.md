<p><p>In the failure analysis equipment market, key types include Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM), Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM), Focused Ion Beams (FIB), and Dual Beam systems. SEM offers high-resolution imaging of surface topographies, crucial for identifying defects. TEM provides insights into internal structures at atomic resolutions. FIB combines milling and imaging capabilities, enabling precise material removal for analysis. Dual Beam systems integrate SEM and FIB, enhancing versatility in failure analysis by allowing simultaneous imaging and sample preparation.</p></p>
