<p><p>The micro fans market is primarily segmented into two types: DC micro fans and AC micro fans. DC micro fans operate on direct current, offering energy efficiency, compact size, and quieter operation, making them ideal for applications in electronics, cooling systems, and computer hardware. In contrast, AC micro fans use alternating current, typically providing higher airflow and durability for industrial applications, such as HVAC systems and appliances. Both types play crucial roles in thermal management and ventilation across various sectors.</p></p>
