<p><p>The disposable intravenous indwelling needle market is categorized into two main types: open indwelling needles and closed indwelling needles. Open indwelling needles allow direct access to the bloodstream and are typically used for short-term procedures. In contrast, closed indwelling needles feature a one-way valve or similar mechanism to minimize the risk of contamination and infection, making them more suitable for prolonged use. These market segments cater to varying medical needs in hospitals, clinics, and outpatient settings.</p></p>
