<p><p>The medical biosensors market encompasses various types based on sensory capabilities. Vision-type biosensors utilize optical sensors to detect biomolecules through visual changes, often employed in glucose monitoring. Hearing-type biosensors rely on sound waves to identify physiological changes, commonly used in ultrasound diagnostics. Smell-type biosensors employ electronic noses to detect volatile compounds, aiding in disease diagnosis and monitoring. Other market types include tactile sensors and multi-modal biosensors, combining various detection methods for comprehensive physiological assessments, enhancing patient care and diagnostics.</p></p>
