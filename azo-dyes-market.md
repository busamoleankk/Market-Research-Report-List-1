<p><ul><li>Disperse dyes</li><li>Metal-complex dyes</li><li>Reactive dyes</li><li>Substantive dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The azo dyes market encompasses various types, each catering to specific applications. Disperse dyes are primarily used in dyeing synthetic fibers, offering vibrant colors. Metal-complex dyes are favored for their excellent lightfastness and wash fastness, suitable for wool and nylon. Reactive dyes chemically bond with fiber, providing an extensive color palette, especially for cotton. Substantive dyes, known for their affinity to protein fibers, are often used in textiles. This diversity enables specialized applications across the textile and other industries.</p></p>
