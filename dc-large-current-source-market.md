<p><p>The DC large current source market is categorized into two main types: unipolarity and bipolarity. Unipolarity refers to systems that provide current in one direction, ideal for applications requiring steady, unidirectional flow, such as electrolysis or plating. In contrast, bipolarity systems can reverse current direction, making them suitable for applications like battery testing or regenerative systems where current needs to fluctuate. Each type serves distinct industrial requirements, influencing design and functionality in electronic applications.</p></p>
