<p><strong>The Digital Print Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital print film market is primarily categorized into solvent-based and water-based films. Solvent-based films utilize organic solvents for ink adhesion, offering vibrant colors and durability, making them suitable for outdoor applications. In contrast, water-based films use water as a solvent, resulting in lower emissions and a more environmentally friendly option. These films are ideal for indoor applications due to their non-toxic nature but may have reduced durability compared to solvent-based alternatives. Both types serve diverse printing needs and preferences.</p></p>
