<p><p>The resin dental cements market consists of various types, primarily including self-adhesive resin dental cements and universal adhesive resin dental cements. Self-adhesive resin cements bond directly to tooth structures and materials without the need for a separate bonding agent, offering ease of use and efficiency. In contrast, universal adhesive resin cements provide versatility, allowing dental professionals to use them with various substrates and bonding systems, making them suitable for multiple applications in restorative dentistry. Both types enhance durability and aesthetics in dental procedures.</p></p>
