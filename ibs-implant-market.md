<p><p>The IBS implant market is primarily divided into two types: screwed and non-screwed implants. Screwed IBS implants involve a threaded design that allows for secure fixation to the jawbone, enhancing stability and allowing for immediate loading in some cases. In contrast, non-screwed IBS implants utilize a friction fit or other retention methods, offering simpler installation and reducing surgical time. Both types cater to different patient needs, preferences, and clinical situations, driving innovation and specialization in dental implant solutions.</p></p>
