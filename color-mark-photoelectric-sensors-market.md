<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Color Mark Photoelectric Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Through-beam Sensors</li><li>Retro-reflective Sensors</li><li>Diffuse-reflective Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Color Mark Photoelectric Sensors market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Through-beam Sensors**: These consist of a light emitter and receiver, detecting objects by an interruption in the beam, ideal for long-range applications.</p><p>2. **Retro-reflective Sensors**: These use a reflector to bounce light back to the sensor, enabling detection of objects without needing to align the emitter and receiver directly.</p><p>3. **Diffuse-reflective Sensors**: These detect objects based on the light they reflect back to the sensor, suitable for shorter ranges and varying surfaces. Each type serves specific application needs in automation and material handling.</p></p>
