<p><ul><li>FEP</li><li>PFA</li><li>PTFE</li><li>PVDF</li><li>ETFE</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fluoropolymer flexible tubing market comprises several types, each offering unique properties. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) is known for its excellent clarity and chemical resistance. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy) provides enhanced thermal stability. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is highly resistant to chemicals and has a low friction coefficient. PVDF (Polyvinylidene Fluoride) offers good mechanical strength and is suitable for various applications. ETFE (Ethylene Tetrafluoroethylene) is durable and resistant to UV and chemicals. The "Others" category includes alternative fluoropolymers with specialized applications.</p></p>
