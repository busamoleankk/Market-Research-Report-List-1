<p><p>The fans and blowers market is categorized into three main types: axial fans and blowers, centrifugal fans and blowers, and others. Axial fans move air parallel to the fan axis and are efficient for low-pressure applications. Centrifugal fans, on the other hand, use a rotating impeller to increase air pressure and are suitable for higher resistance systems. Other types may include specialty fans like mixed flow or diaphragm fans, catering to specific industrial or commercial needs. Each type serves distinct airflow and pressure requirements.</p></p>
