The FRAX tool estimates fracture risk based on clinical risk factors and bone density. CT scans offer detailed 3D images of bones but are less commonly used. X-rays can identify fractures, while ultrasounds provide a non-invasive option for assessing bone quality. Other methods may include biochemical markers and physical assessments, enhancing overall osteoporosis risk evaluation.</p></p>
