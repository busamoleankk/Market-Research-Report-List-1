<p><strong>The Locking Differential Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Automatic Locking Differential</li><li>Selectable Locking Differential</li><li>Spool</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Locking differentials enhance traction by allowing both wheels on an axle to turn at the same speed. In automatic locking differentials, the system engages when detecting wheel slip, providing seamless traction without driver input. Selectable locking differentials allow drivers to manually engage or disengage the lock, offering versatility for different terrains. Spool differentials provide maximum traction as they keep both wheels locked together permanently, ideal for racing or off-roading but limiting maneuverability. Each type serves specific needs for various driving conditions.</p></p>
