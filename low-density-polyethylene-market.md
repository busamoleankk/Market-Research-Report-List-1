<p><p>The Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE) market is primarily segmented into two production processes: the autoclave and tubular processes. The autoclave process involves polymerizing ethylene in a controlled environment, allowing for tight molecular weight control, resulting in high clarity and flexibility. The tubular process, on the other hand, uses a continuous extrusion method, offering higher production rates and efficiency. Both methods cater to various applications in packaging, containers, and films, driving the demand for LDPE in diverse industries.</p></p>
