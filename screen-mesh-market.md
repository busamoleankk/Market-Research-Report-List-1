<p><p>The screen mesh market encompasses various types based on operational mechanisms: Gravity Type, Vacuum Type, and Pressurized Type. </p><p>Gravity Type utilizes gravity to facilitate material flow through screens, suitable for coarse materials. Vacuum Type employs suction to enhance efficiency in separating finer particles and minimizing dust. Pressurized Type uses air pressure to propel materials through the mesh, ensuring optimal particle separation and minimizing clogging. Each type caters to specific applications across industries, including mining, food processing, and chemical manufacturing, enhancing productivity and processing efficiency.</p></p>
