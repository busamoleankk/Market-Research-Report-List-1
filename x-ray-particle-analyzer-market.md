<p><strong>The X-ray Particle Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-ray Diffraction Type</li><li>X-ray Fluorescence Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray Particle Analyzer market consists of two primary types: X-ray Diffraction (XRD) and X-ray Fluorescence (XRF). XRD measures the crystalline structure of materials by analyzing the diffraction patterns of X-rays, providing information on phase identification and crystallinity. In contrast, XRF determines the elemental composition of materials by measuring the fluorescent X-rays emitted from a sample when exposed to a primary X-ray source. Both techniques are essential in various industries for material characterization and quality control.</p></p>
