<p><ul><li>Fixed and Floating Flange</li><li>End Welding</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel axial expansion joint market comprises two primary types: fixed flange and floating flange, as well as end welding joints. Fixed flange joints offer rigidity and are designed to accommodate axial movements while preventing lateral displacement. Floating flange joints, on the other hand, allow for slight misalignments, providing flexibility in installation and operation. End welding joints are characterized by welded connections that provide a seamless and strong integration in piping systems, enhancing overall durability and performance.</p></p>
