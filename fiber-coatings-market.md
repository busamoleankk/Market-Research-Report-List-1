<p><p>The fiber coatings market encompasses various types, including optical fiber coatings, fiberglass coatings, and carbon fiber coatings. Optical fiber coatings enhance the performance of light-transmitting fibers, providing durability and reducing signal loss. Fiberglass coatings protect and reinforce glass fibers used in construction and automotive applications, improving their strength and resistance to environmental factors. Carbon fiber coatings are designed to enhance the properties of carbon fibers, offering lightweight strength and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them ideal for aerospace and sports equipment.</p></p>
