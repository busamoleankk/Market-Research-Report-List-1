<p><ul><li>Partially Absorbable Hernia Repair Mesh</li><li>Fully Absorbable Hernia Repair Mesh</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Biologic Hernia Repair Mesh market comprises two main types: partially absorbable and fully absorbable hernia repair mesh. Partially absorbable meshes are designed to provide initial support while gradually being absorbed by the body, allowing natural tissue regeneration. In contrast, fully absorbable meshes are intended to dissolve completely after promoting healing, eliminating long-term foreign material in the body. Both types are used to enhance recovery and improve outcomes in hernia repair surgeries, catering to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
