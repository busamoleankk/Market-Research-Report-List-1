<p><ul><li>Drum</li><li>Roller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The firewood cleaner market primarily features two types: drum and roller cleaners. Drum firewood cleaners utilize a cylindrical design to efficiently remove dirt and debris from firewood through a rotating mechanism, ensuring thorough cleaning. In contrast, roller firewood cleaners employ multiple rollers that gently agitate the wood, effectively dislodging unwanted particles while minimizing damage to the logs. Both types are designed for enhancing firewood quality, improving burning efficiency, and ensuring better safety during the burning process.</p></p>
