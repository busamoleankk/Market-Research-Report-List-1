<p><p>Electric pet fence systems are designed to keep pets safe within designated boundaries and are categorized into two main types: in-ground fences and wireless fences. In-ground fences consist of buried wires that create a defined perimeter, offering customizable shapes and sizes to suit various landscapes. Conversely, wireless fences use a central transmitter to create a circular boundary, allowing for easier installation and portability. Both systems utilize a collar that emits a warning or correction signal when pets approach the defined limits.</p></p>
