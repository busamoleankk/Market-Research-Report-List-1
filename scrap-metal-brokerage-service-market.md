<p><p>The scrap metal brokerage service market is divided into two main types: ferrous and non-ferrous scrap metal brokerage. Ferrous scrap metal brokerage focuses on materials containing iron, such as steel and cast iron, which are commonly recycled for manufacturing and construction purposes. Non-ferrous scrap metal brokerage, on the other hand, deals with metals that do not contain iron, like aluminum, copper, and brass, valued for their lightweight and resistance to corrosion. Both markets facilitate the collection and sale of recyclable metals.</p></p>
