<p><ul><li>Unipolar</li><li>Bipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high voltage amplifier market is generally segmented into unipolar and bipolar types. Unipolar amplifiers operate with a single power supply, delivering voltage output in one direction, making them simpler for specific applications. In contrast, bipolar amplifiers utilize both positive and negative power supplies, allowing for output in both directions, which enhances their versatility in dealing with AC signals. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like telecommunications, biomedical, and industrial automation, catering to varying voltage requirements and performance needs.</p></p>
