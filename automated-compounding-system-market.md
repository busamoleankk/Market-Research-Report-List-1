<p><p>The Automated Compounding System (ACS) market includes two primary types: Gravimetric and Volumetric systems. Gravimetric Automated Compounding Systems utilize weight measurements to ensure precise ingredient quantities, enhancing accuracy in pharmaceutical compounding. In contrast, Volumetric Automated Compounding Systems measure ingredient volumes for formulation. Both systems aim to improve efficiency, reduce human error, and maintain consistency in drug preparation, catering to hospitals, pharmacies, and compounding centers by streamlining workflows and ensuring patient safety through reliable drug formulation processes.</p></p>
